About SASF tournament

The Sudanese American Soccer Federation (SASF) Tournament is an annual soccer tournament held by the Sudanese American Federation.Its purpose is to have an annual get together for Sudanese American's across the country and to create lasting connections amongst the members. It is held in a different state every year.
