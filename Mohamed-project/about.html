About SASF Tournament

The Sudanese American Soccer Federation (SASF) Tournament is an annual soccer tournament held by the Sudanese American Federation. Its purpose is to have an annual get-together for Sudanese Americans across the country and to create lasting connections among the members. It is held in a different state every year.
